Social Security

Helping you navigate Social Security benefits

Ring & Associates PC will handle your Social Security Disability claim from the beginning and help you through every stage of your claim process. With a combined legal experience of over 50 years, our team is ready to help you get the compensation you deserve through aggressive representation and friendly assistance. A well-trained, professional staff such as the team at Ring & Associates PC will be the difference between winning your claim and having your claim rejected. If your claim has been denied at the initial stage, our staff will determine the best way to handle your appeal and represent you at a Social Security hearing. Our team is committed to your success. Contact us today for a no obligation consultation.

Specializing in Social Security litigation

Has your claimed for SSDI or SSI been denied for an unjust cause? Seeking the help of a lawyer that specializes in Social Security litigation is your answer. Our team will help you understand your claim, why it was denied and how to effectively provide evidence that proves you are entitled to benefits such as short and long term disability.
